Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 9, 2026

Experience Wholesale ("we," "us," or "our") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you interact with our SMS-based vehicle acquisition service.

1. Information We Collect

When you contact us or submit a vehicle for consideration, we may collect:

  • Your name and phone number
  • Vehicle details (year, make, model, VIN, mileage)
  • Photos you send of the vehicle
  • The content of SMS messages exchanged with us
  • Asking price and any notes you provide

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information collected solely to:

  • Evaluate and respond to vehicle purchase inquiries
  • Send bid offers and follow-up communications via SMS
  • Process and complete vehicle acquisition transactions
  • Maintain records of our business interactions

3. SMS Messaging

By texting us or submitting a vehicle inquiry, you consent to receive SMS messages from Experience Wholesale related to your submission. Message and data rates may apply. You may opt out at any time by replying STOP to any message. Reply HELP for assistance.

4. Information Sharing

We do not sell, rent, trade, or share your personal information with third parties for marketing or advertising purposes. Your information is used exclusively for the purpose of evaluating and completing vehicle transactions with Experience Wholesale.

We may share information only when required by law or to protect our legal rights.

5. Data Retention

We retain your information for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes described in this policy and to comply with applicable legal obligations.

6. Security

We take reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect your information from unauthorized access, loss, or misuse.
